PokerGrid Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 June 2026

PokerGrid is a mobile card strategy game developed by Bald Knight Games. This privacy policy explains how data is handled in the app.

Information We Collect

PokerGrid does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal contact information.

Advertising

PokerGrid uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers, advertising data, usage data, diagnostics, and approximate location, depending on the user’s device settings and consent choices.

In-App Purchases

PokerGrid offers optional non-consumable in-app purchases, including Remove Ads and Unlock All Themes. Purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. PokerGrid does not receive or store payment card details.

Game Center

PokerGrid may use Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards. Game Center data is managed by Apple according to the user's Apple account and Game Center settings.

Children

PokerGrid is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

PokerGrid does not sell personal data. Third-party services such as Google AdMob and Apple services may process data according to their own privacy policies.
